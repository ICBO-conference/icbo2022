Location

The 2022 International Conference of Biomedical Ontology (ICBO 2022) will be held in 2022 at the Michigan League, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI, USA.

Michigan League Address: 911 North University, Ann Arbor, MI 48109.

Conference Dates

  • September 25-28, 2022 (Sunday-Wednesday)
  • Workshops: September 25-26, 2022
  • Main conference: September 27-28, 2022

This meeting will be held in a hybrid format that includes both in-person and virtual participation and is part of the Michigan Week of Ontology and Semantic Web.

Keynote Speakers

  • Chris Stoeckert (University of Pennsylvania)
  • Susan Gregurick (National Institutes of Health)
